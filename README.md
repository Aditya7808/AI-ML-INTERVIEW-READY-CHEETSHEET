## üí° Common Interview Questions

These resources cover answers to frequently asked interview questions such as:
- **Boosting Algorithms**: How does Adaboost differ from XGboost? What are their pros and cons?
- **ML Pipeline**: What are the steps in a typical ML pipeline?
- **Data Handling**: How do you handle missing data, outliers, and imbalanced datasets?
- **Evaluation**: What evaluation metrics are most important for different use cases (classification, regression)?
- **Overfitting**: How do you prevent overfitting and ensure model generalization?
- **Data Splitting**: Why is proper train-test splitting critical? What about cross-validation?
- **Feature Engineering**: How do you select, scale, and create meaningful features?
- **Model Selection**: How do you choose the right algorithm for your problem?
- **Hyperparameter Tuning**: What strategies work best for optimization?
